## Why These References Matter

The desired Pawbie direction is closer to Baggu + Wild One than to a typical tech product:

- Baggu: elevated lifestyle, retro saturation, bold graphic composition, black as a confident anchor.
- Wild One: pet/lifestyle collage, oversized typography, playful product-world energy, geometric color blocking.
- Jellycat: useful lesson is not palette breadth, but the presence of a recognizable brand blue across many product categories.

## Baggu Read

Baggu's brand color system does not behave like one fixed color. It behaves like:

- black wordmark / typography as the stable brand anchor
- warm off-white canvas
- seasonal illustration and print palettes
- bold but slightly retro colors, rarely glossy or techy

| Role | Approx Hex | Note |
|------|------------|------|
| Warm canvas | `#F5F2EC` | Off-white page ground |
| Black anchor | `#000000` | Logo, nav, giant typography |
| Dusty periwinkle | `#6A77B1` | Dolphin body |
| Pale lavender | `#C1BCDB` | Large background block |
| Brick orange | `#D44D2B` | Retro pop, not neon |
| Muted shadow | `#5B4B58` | Outline/depth |

Takeaway for Pawbie:

- Pawbie can use a strong dark anchor more confidently.
- A single brand color does not have to carry everything; a stable typographic/graphic system can do a lot.
- The palette can be more saturated than the current packaging, as long as saturation is earthy/retro rather than neon.

## Wild One Read

Wild One appears to have a stable black wordmark, but the brand world is built through large color blocks, collage assets, and rotating product/campaign palettes.

- Pawbie can borrow collage grammar, but should lower the energy of the chartreuse/yellow unless it is a campaign moment.
- The pet-category cue comes less from paw prints and more from product-world composition.

## Jellycat Lesson

Jellycat's strongest lesson for Pawbie is category coherence: many characters and many product categories can still feel unified if there is one stable brand color or brand mark behavior.

For Pawbie, the missing piece may be a stronger core brand color. Current colors are warm and soft, but not yet distinctive enough as a brand signature.

## What Is the Brand Color?

### Baggu

From the provided reference, Baggu's effective brand anchor is black + warm off-white, not a single expressive color. The color world changes by print/campaign.

### Wild One

From the provided reference, Wild One's effective brand anchor is also black + warm off-white, with campaign/product colors doing the expressive work. In this screenshot, blue, chartreuse, green, cocoa red, and pale pink all appear as campaign colors rather than one fixed brand color.

### Pawbie Opportunity

Pawbie probably needs one stable, ownable brand color in addition to its three fur colors.

The current `Clay #8A7B6A` is warm and useful, but it is too neutral to be a strong brand signature. The packaging and character art already point toward a soft Pawbie blue.

## Suggested Pawbie Color Strategy

Use a two-layer color system:

1. Stable brand anchors
   - Pawbie Blue candidate: start testing `#9BC7DF` or `#7799C0`
   - Graphite / near-black anchor: `#1C1C1E` or `#111111`
   - Warm package ground: `#F6EDE4`

2. Rotating lifestyle palette
   - Fur Pink `#FFC5E6`
   - Fur Cream `#FCFCF7`
   - Fur Blue `#D8E5F4`
   - Brick / cocoa accent
   - Muted green
   - Occasional butter/chartreuse only for campaign energy

## Recommendation

The most promising direction is:

```text
Pawbie Blue as the signature color
+ Baggu-like black/warm canvas confidence
+ Wild One-like collage and color blocking
+ Pawbie packaging cream/blue band softness
```

Test these three candidate signature blues next:

| Candidate | Hex | Personality |
|-----------|-----|-------------|
| Packaging Blue | `#9BC7DF` | Soft, closest to current logo/package |
| Lifestyle Blue | `#7799C0` | More mature, pet/lifestyle |
| Periwinkle Blue | `#7484BF` | Most Baggu-like, graphic/editorial |

Do not choose until tested on:

- logo lockup
- packaging front panel
- landing hero
- app primary action / selected state
- social card cover
- three-Pawbie switcher
