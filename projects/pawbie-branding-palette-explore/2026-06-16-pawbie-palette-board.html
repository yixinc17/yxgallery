Pawbie Palette Exploration

Palette report for the revised Pawbie shortlist. The goal is to separate brand expression from app usability: neutral UI foundation, controlled brand accents, richer color only in illustration and campaign layers.

Report Result

07r Maillard Reduced Red is the current working baseline. The other revised routes remain comparison references. App screens use readable cream/oat surfaces and graphite structure; Kraft/Cocoa carry warmth; red stays a small semantic signal.

App Color Rule

Use about 70% warm neutral UI base, 20% graphite/clay structure, 5% Pawbie identity signal, and 5% campaign or character accents. Brand colors should support recognition without becoming the whole interface.

Key Color Hypothesis

The app key color should not default to Fur Blue. Pawbie has pink, cream, and blue product variants, so the system key should sit outside the fur trio: Graphite for actions, Clay/Kraft for warmth, and Eye Teal only as a life/intelligence signal.

Shortlist

01 Packaging Blue

Closest to current packaging and logo softness. Concern: can drift into baby cotton-brand territory; mature it with graphite, stronger type, and earthy accents.

02 Lifestyle Blue

Mature pet-lifestyle blue. Strong candidate for a Pawbie signature color.

03 Periwinkle Blue

Most Baggu-like and editorial. Stylish, but may pull away from current Pawbie softness.

Shortlist

04 Collage Grammar

Illustration color feels comfortable. In Web/App, keep the full palette inside illustrations, stickers, modules, and pattern moments; UI chrome should stay restrained.

05 Eye Teal

Uses real eye gradient as the brand life/intelligence signal. More distinctive than soft blue.

06 Olive Lifestyle

Non-blue core direction. Good for pet/life/diary adjacency, calmer and more grown-up.

Shortlist

07 Terracotta Cocoa

Warm Maillard-like direction. Keep red/cocoa area smaller: mostly labels, strips, typography, and package structure rather than full backgrounds.

08 Plum Mauve

Retro and sophisticated. Harmonizes with pink without becoming candy-like.

Shortlist

09 Butter Cornflower

Bright and useful, but colorful areas should be smaller. Good for campaign accents, launch banners, badges, and social rather than core App surfaces.

Shortlist

10 Neutral Clay Charcoal

Most design-object direction and strongest core candidate. Lets the eye gradient and fur colors become emotional accents, not the whole brand.
