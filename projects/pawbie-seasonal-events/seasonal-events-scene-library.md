# Pawbie Seasonal, Holiday, And Major-Moment Scene Library

## Product Fidelity Anchor

Use the new physical product reference as the strongest visual anchor:

- Three-color family: white, pink, pale blue.
- Pink is the hero SKU and should usually sit center or foreground.
- Body shape is a rounded-square fluffy blob, not a perfect sphere.
- Fur is fluffy and slightly droopy near the bottom.
- Eyes are glossy blue-cyan electronic screens; expressions can change.
- Nose is a small black rounded camera-like dot.
- Feet are small solid oval feet in a gray-white silicone material, understated and not transparent.
- Pawbie should feel like a soft desktop electronic life, not a generic plush animal.
